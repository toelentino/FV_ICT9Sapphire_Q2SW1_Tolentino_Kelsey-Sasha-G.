About Tekken

Tekken is a popular fighting game franchise developed and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The series is known for its diverse cast of characters, each with unique fighting styles and backstories. The first game was released in 1994, and it has since become one of the best-selling video game franchises of all time.

The gameplay focuses on one-on-one battles, where players can choose from a variety of characters, each with their own special moves and combos. The series has evolved over the years, introducing new characters, mechanics, and storylines that keep fans engaged.
